About Me

I am Web Analyst based in London, although my heart is Italian by birth I am a wandering soul who finds fulfillment in travelling. My skills range from coding(Techdegree certified) to dashboarding, passing through the multifaceted world of Web analytics from implementation to reporting. Tool agnostic both in tagging and analytics, I am predominately a Google Analytics and Adobe Analytics analyst as well as a GTM, DTM and Tealium adept. A fast learner and a proactive worker who likes to work in a team and thrive on solo job adventures.
